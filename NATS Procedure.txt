[NATS Preflight and Planning]
Refer OM C 6.8.1.1.2 
OFP TMI No.
ETOPS Entry and Exit.
Full Oceanic Waypoints.
Weather : Destination, Destination Alternates, Enroute Temp and Forecast.

Tech Log. ETOPS check signed.
MEL items affects ETOPS, PBN, RVSM, COMMS/NAV/SURV (CNS), PBCS Capabilities, Clock Accuracy.
Updated Weather.


[NATS Enroute]
Refer OM C 6.8.1.1.2
1. FMC/CPDLC : 
ETOPS Segment (434). Check weather and alternate.

2. Check Entry Point, Exit Point.
Verify waypoint, speed and FL (climb if necessary). 
Use OFP Lat Long in OFP to crosscheck track and distance.

3.Set Oceanic Clearance in CPDLC.
Bodo ENOB : 20 mins
Gander CZQX : 90 - 60 mins
Shanwick EGGX : 90 - 30 mins
Santa Maria LPPO : at least 40 mins
NY Oceanic East : no requirement RCL. Received during Departure PDC.

4. Preset HF Frequency.
5. Log ON CPDLC (10-25 mins). SELCAL check.
6. Clearance Received. Verify routing, ETA, FL and Mach No.

After Entry:
1. Check RNP 4
2. Set 10 mins :reset SQ2000 (Except BIRD : maintain SQ)
3. Confirm uplink route.
4. VHF 1 and 2 Comm : 123.45, 121.5
5. Maintain ECON Spd unless intructed by ATC.
6. SLOP.
7. Altimeter Check.

Waypoint Check :
1. Confirm LNAV. Check tracking.
2. Log OFP.
3. HF Call is any.
4. Check position report sent in FMC (after 10 mins cross check the waypoint)
5. After 10 mins, check position aircraft symbol.

At Boundary
1. HF Call and SELCAL Check
2. Confirm CPDLC has transferred.

[NATS Exit]
1. Confirm routing and speed.
2. Cancel SLOP.
3. Confirm Transponder
